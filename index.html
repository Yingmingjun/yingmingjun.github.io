Self Introduction

Hi! I am a final-year undergraduate at Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, supervised by Prof. Yongjun Xu. I am currently applying for a Ph.D. in Wireless Communication (Fall 2023). My motivation to study a communication engineering major is facilitate access for the global population, providing opportunities regardless of age, gender, race, ethnicity, religion, disability or any other distinguishing characteristics.

The year 2019 marked a crucial transition in my life: on a personal level, I had just become a university student, but more importantly, it was the year when the 5G first hit the ground running in China, opening an unprecedented new era. However, this futuristic vision will only be possible if the 5G can be deployed successfully in an affordable manner. That is why academia has been exploring cutting-edge solutions to resource allocation problems to lower costs and bring the fully connected world to life for all potential users. From my perspective, I would embrace the opportunity to participate in the development of 5G technology.
